Gift Centre:
Description:
Gift Centre is an app where you can find the best gifts for your babies, especially Diapers, First Birthday, Special & Imported Toys and Cakes for you babies. A complete guide how to choose best gift for your baby.

Features:
•	Fully research based application on Babies Gift.
•	The Research results on the How to choose best gift on desired stage for babies.
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	 Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Searching feature.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing).
